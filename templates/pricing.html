Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time and your billing will be prorated. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a guided proof-of-concept for qualified organizations. Contact us for a personalized demo and trial discussion.

What SIEM platforms are supported?

R2Purple integrates with Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Elastic Security, QRadar, and more. Custom integrations are available on Enterprise plans.

Is R2Purple safe for production environments?

Yes. All tests are designed to be non-destructive. Agents operate with minimal footprint and can be scoped to specific hosts and techniques.

Not sure which plan is right for you?

Our team can help you choose the right plan based on your environment size and security goals.
